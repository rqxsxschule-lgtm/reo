Common Questions

How do I add the bot to my server? Visit the home page and click Add to Server. You must have server administrator permissions to authorize the bot.

Bot is offline or not responding? Check the status of the bot in your server. If issues persist, contact support through the community or email.

How do I report bugs or suggest features? Join our support community to report issues and submit feature requests directly to the development team.

Is there documentation available? Detailed command documentation and setup guides are available in the support community server.
